PCG Environment | UE5 & PCG

Game Information


This project was created in Unreal Engine using Procedural Content Generation (PCG) and Landmass. PCG allowed me to randomize the placement of trees, rocks, and other elements to achieve a natural and coherent look for the scene. I used Landmass to surround the scene and give it the appearance of a village lost in the mountains.
